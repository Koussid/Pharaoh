Few Words About Us

Pharaoh was founded in Early 2020, We are a technology company that is building the material movement of India through a globally unique innovation of tech trucking, enabled by a strong interplay of technology, data, culture and operational excellence. Our mission is to ensure each fleet owner, the pilot, and the loaders a satisfied price range by eliminating middlemen and also to eleminate their pain point with our tech. We are solving the full range of challenges of the trucking industry using technology — be it complex problems like fuel analytics, route planning, human behaviour analysis or pure-drudgery elimination tasks like auto-alert systems and intelligent decision systems. Our technology obsession has resulted in simple, intuitive technology products gaining quick and easy adoption by the trucking ecosystem stakeholders. We are contributing to building the new India by defining the next-generation trucking industry. With accurate predictions, high levels of efficiency, simple automated operations, and a strong and pure mission, we are re-imagining logistics for India.
